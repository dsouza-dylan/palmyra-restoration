# 🐟 The Palmyra Restoration Experiment

An interactive data visualization tracking five coral restoration strategies across a decade of annual surveys at Palmyra Atoll, including through the 2023–2024 global coral bleaching event.

Built with D3.js, Leaflet, and Scrollama. Data from annual photogrammetry surveys processed using structure-from-motion (SfM) and Virtual Point Intercept (VPI) analysis.

Developed by Dylan Dsouza in the Smith Lab at Scripps Institution of Oceanography, UC San Diego. Special thanks to Mitchell Smelser, Samantha Clements, Dr. Adi Khen, Dr. Beverly French, and Dr. Jennifer Smith. Research supported by The Nature Conservancy and the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service.
